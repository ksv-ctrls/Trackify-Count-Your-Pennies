About Count Your Pennies.

Count Your Pennies is a user friendly web app that helps you keep track of your expenses.

With Count Your Pennies, you can :

  • Count and Track each one of your pennies
  • End-to-end encryption for all of your data
  • Seamless integration and data movement
  • A growing global community

We believe that tracking your expenses should be simple, secure, and accessible to everyone. Count Your Pennies is designed to provide a seamless experience whether your expense is hard to track or hard to keep in mind.
